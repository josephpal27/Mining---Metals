Mining & Metals

Mining and metals refer to two interconnected industries involved in the extraction of minerals and ores from the earth and the subsequent processing of these raw materials into metals and other useful products. Mining involves the extraction of valuable minerals or other geological materials from the earth, usually from an ore body, vein, or coal seam. Once mined, ores undergo various processes to extract the valuable metals they contain. This typically involves crushing and grinding the ore to liberate the desired minerals, followed by physical or chemical separation techniques to concentrate the metal-bearing minerals.
